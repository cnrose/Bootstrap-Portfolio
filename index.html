About Me

I am a Kansas native who is currently residing in the Atlanta-metro with my husband and eight cats (yes, eight. It's a long story..)

Previously I studied Classical Languages, graduating from the University of Kansas (rock chalk!) with a B.A. and from the University of Massachussetts-Amherst with an M.A.T. in Latin. I taught high school Latin for 4 years before ultimately deciding I was ready for a new challenge. And so, I am currently studying web development in the Georgia Tech Coding Bootcamp.

When my eyes aren't glued to the computer screen, you can find me reading a page-turning physchological thriller, hiking on the North Georgia trails, or napping on the couch (a hobby I may have picked up from all those cats??).
